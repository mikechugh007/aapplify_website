Why AAPPLIFY

Built to prove AI-agent actions were controlled.

AAPPLIFY exists because AI agents are becoming enterprise actors, but the control evidence around their access, data use, tool calls, exceptions, and remediation is scattered across identity, security, data, change, and workflow systems.

Why we exist

Most teams already have alerts, tickets, policies, scanners, identity systems, workflow tools, and GRC programs. What they lack is consistent proof that the right controls operated at the right time for AI agents, automations, and delegated identities. AAPPLIFY starts there, then extends the same evidence model to risky access, data movement, cloud exposure, production change, and third-party activity.

What we believe

The operating principles behind the product are practical on purpose.

Context before automation

Workflows should be triggered by evidence, ownership, policy, and control context, not by isolated alerts.

Team focus, shared evidence

Security, identity, data, compliance, and platform teams need workflows that match their ownership without losing a common evidence record.

Controls by design

Policies, approvals, guardrails, and audit evidence should be part of the operating model from the start.

Use the systems teams trust

AAPPLIFY connects existing tools and files instead of asking teams to replace every workflow on day one.

Fast time to value

The first job is to prove value around one high-priority risk workflow, then expand by department or control area.

Who we build for

  • Security teams that need AI-agent exposure, posture, and remediation signals connected to action.
  • Identity teams that need to prove agent access was owned, scoped, approved, and time-bound.
  • Data and compliance teams that need policy-aware evidence for AI-agent access to sensitive data.
  • Platform, release, and application teams that need agent production readiness tied to controls and ownership.

What customers can expect

We start with a focused pilot around one AI-agent control workflow: the systems that create the signal, the owners who make the decision, the remediation or exception path, and the evidence needed to prove control.

Team

AAPPLIFY is built by a small team focused on AI Agent Control Assurance and the broader evidence model for high-risk enterprise actions.
