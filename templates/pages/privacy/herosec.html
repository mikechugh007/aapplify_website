Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Overview

AAPPLIFY collects and uses information to operate our website, respond to demo requests, onboard customers, and support the enterprise control-plane platform. This Privacy Policy explains the categories of information we collect through the public website and how that information is used, shared, and protected.

2. Information we collect

  • Demo and contact information: Name, work email, company, role, and any control-plane workflow details you submit through forms or scheduling tools.
  • Customer onboarding information: Business contact details, implementation notes, and information needed to scope integrations, pilots, and subscriptions.
  • Website and product usage data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, timestamps, and similar diagnostic information.
  • Integration and telemetry data: Information available through connected identity, change, data, security, engineering, and platform systems as described in applicable customer agreements.

3. How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries, schedule demos, and communicate about pilots or subscriptions.
  • Operate, secure, maintain, and improve the website and platform.
  • Scope integrations, onboard customers, and support control-plane workflows.
  • Analyze usage patterns, reliability, and security events.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Vendors that help us host the website, manage scheduling, send communications, monitor performance, or support the platform.
  • Customer-requested integrations: Third-party systems connected at a customer's direction and governed by the customer's relationship with those providers.
  • Legal and security needs: Parties involved in legal process, fraud prevention, security response, or protection of our rights and users.
  • Corporate transactions: A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

5. Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the website, respond to inquiries, support customer relationships, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Customer data retention for the platform may also be governed by customer contracts or data-processing terms.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure website usage, improve performance, and support security. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

8. Your choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time. To make a request, contact support@aapplify.com.

9. Third-party services

The website may include links to or embedded services provided by third parties, such as scheduling tools. Those third parties control their own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies directly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version here and update the last-updated date above.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how AAPPLIFY handles information, contact support@aapplify.com.
