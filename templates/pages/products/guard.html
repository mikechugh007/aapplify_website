What Guard does

Exception governance for AI-agent actions.

Guard is not a generic monitoring page or anomaly detector. It is the control workflow for accepted risk: who approved it, why it was accepted, what compensating control applies, when it expires, and what evidence proves the exception was governed.

Temporary access

Track short-lived access for AI agents, service accounts, tools, APIs, sensitive data, or production workflows.

Compensating controls

Attach the control that makes the exception acceptable, such as scoped permissions, logging, review, or time limits.

Renewal decisions

Route renew, revoke, remediate, escalate, or approve decisions before temporary risk becomes stale risk.

Audit-ready evidence

Keep approval, justification, policy, owner, expiration, and closure evidence together for audit and board review.
