What is Connect?

AAPPLIFY Connect is your AI-native ingestion and normalization layer built for operational use, not just data movement. Leveraging advanced machine learning, it automatically adapts to new data sources, detects anomalies, and ensures high-quality, trusted data for downstream detection and automation. Connect turns scattered telemetry, tickets, spreadsheets, and database records into a consistent, action-ready stream that powers your business intelligence, automation, and AI workflows.
